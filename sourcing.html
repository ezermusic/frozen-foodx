Storage & Preservation

Storage & Preservation Standards

Our facility maintains strict temperature zones for different product categories — ensuring optimal preservation from the moment products arrive.

-18°C

Deep Frozen Storage

Poultry, red meat, and seafood are stored at -18°C or below to prevent bacterial growth and preserve nutritional quality.

-12°C

Standard Frozen Zone

Suitable for products with shorter shelf requirements while still maintaining full frozen integrity and product safety.

0–4°C

Chilled Storage

For thaw-ready or fresh-delivery products awaiting same-day or next-day distribution to clients.

24/7

Continuous Monitoring

Temperature loggers and alarm systems run continuously to ensure no cold-chain breach goes undetected at any hour.
