    Our Story

    A Legacy of Freshness & Reliability

    Abak Company Limited is a proudly family-owned business established in 2016, built on a strong foundation of trust, quality, and commitment to excellence. Since our incorporation, we have grown steadily to become a reliable supplier in the frozen food industry.

    We specialize in the importation and distribution of high-quality frozen products, including beef, poultry, pork, and a variety of fish. Our sourcing process is carefully managed to ensure that all products meet strict quality and safety standards, giving our customers confidence in every purchase.

    At Abak Company Limited, we understand the importance of consistency, freshness, and affordability. Whether serving wholesalers, retailers, or food service providers, we are dedicated to delivering products that meet the needs of our diverse clientele. As a family-run business, we place great value on long-term relationships, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is not just to supply frozen foods, but to be a trusted partner in your business and household needs.

    Today, Abak serves restaurants, hotels, caterers, retailers and households across Ghana, delivering not just products, but trust, consistency and excellence.
