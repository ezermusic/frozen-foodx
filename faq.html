Products

6 Questions
We sell a wide range of premium imported frozen foods, organised into three main categories:
  • Poultry: Whole chicken, chicken parts (wings, thighs, drumsticks, breasts), turkey, and other poultry products.
  • Fish & Seafood: Atlantic salmon, mackerel, tilapia, herring, sardines, tuna, squid, shrimp/prawns, and other seafood.
  • Red Meat: Beef cuts, lamb, pork, and mixed meat options for restaurants and households.
All products are internationally sourced and held to the highest quality standards.
🌍 Global Sourcing
Our products are sourced from leading exporting countries including the United States (premium poultry), Brazil (high-volume meat and chicken), Netherlands (seafood), China (seafood and poultry), Norway (Atlantic salmon and seafood), and India (shrimp and tilapia). Each supplier is verified for compliance with international food safety standards before we import.
Many of our poultry and meat products are sourced from halal-certified suppliers. Halal certification is indicated on product packaging. If you require halal-certified products specifically, please let our sales team know when placing your order and we will confirm availability. Call us on +233 24 729 8551 for details.
We serve both individuals and businesses. Our primary clientele includes restaurants, hotels, caterers, and retailers who purchase in bulk — but we also sell to households who wish to purchase frozen foods in reasonable quantities. Contact us to discuss your needs and we will advise on the best options for you.
📅 Shelf Life
Shelf life varies by product type and is printed on each product's packaging. As a general guide:
  • Poultry: 12–18 months when stored at -18°C or below
  • Seafood: 12–24 months depending on the species
  • Red Meat: 12–18 months when properly frozen
We always ensure products have sufficient remaining shelf life when dispatched.
Yes, for qualified business clients considering large orders, we may be able to arrange product samples. Please speak to our sales team directly to discuss this. Contact us at +233 50 496 7111 or via WhatsApp to make a request.

Cold Storage

5 Questions
Yes. In addition to importing and distributing frozen foods, Abak Company Limited offers cold storage services to businesses that need to store frozen or perishable goods. This is ideal for importers, food manufacturers, caterers, and distributors who require reliable, temperature-controlled storage. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
Our facility maintains multiple temperature zones:
  • 🧊 Deep Frozen (−18°C or below): For poultry, red meat, and most seafood
  • ❄️ Standard Frozen (−12°C): For shorter shelf-life frozen products
  • 🌡️ Chilled (0°C – 4°C): For thaw-ready or fresh-delivery products
Temperature is monitored 24/7 using digital loggers and alarm systems to ensure no breach goes undetected.
✅ 24/7 Monitoring
Yes. Our cold storage facility operates with continuous temperature monitoring — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Automated alarm systems alert our team immediately if any temperature deviation occurs, ensuring rapid response before any product quality is compromised.

Quality & Safety

5 Questions
We enforce a multi-layered quality assurance process:
  • ✅ Supplier Verification: Only suppliers meeting international food safety standards (HACCP, ISO 22000) are approved
  • ✅ Incoming Inspection: Every shipment is visually and temperature-checked on arrival
  • ✅ Packaging Integrity Check: Damaged or compromised packaging is rejected
  • ✅ Batch Tracking: All products are tracked with arrival date, origin, and expiry
  • ✅ Document Verification: Health certificates and compliance documents are reviewed for every consignment
🇬🇭 Compliant
Yes. Abak Company Limited operates in compliance with the Ghana Food & Drugs Authority (FDA) requirements for imported frozen foods. We are a licensed importer operating within Ghana's regulatory framework. All products we bring in meet the applicable FDA standards for importation and sale in Ghana.
📦 Returns
In the rare event that you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately and do not consume or discard the product before speaking to us. Reach us on +233 24 729 8551 or +233 50 496 7111. We will investigate and arrange a resolution, which may include replacement or refund depending on the circumstances.
Our suppliers are required to conduct microbiological and chemical testing of their products and provide documentation as part of our supplier compliance requirements. Additionally, all products entering Ghana undergo regulatory inspection processes. We take contamination prevention extremely seriously across our entire supply chain.
Yes. Abak Company Limited is a proud member of the American Chamber of Commerce Ghana (AmCham Ghana), which reflects our commitment to international business standards, transparency, and best practices in trade and food safety.
