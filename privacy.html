Privacy Policy

📅 Effective Date: 1 January 2026 🔄 Last Updated: January 2026 🏢 Document: PP-001
This Privacy Policy explains how Abak Company Limited ("Abak," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you interact with our business, website, or services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and handling your data responsibly.
§1

Overview & Our Commitment

Abak Company Limited is a registered Ghanaian business engaged in the importation, distribution, and cold storage of premium frozen food products. Our principal place of business is located at HQ1 Anunsah Clottey Street, Kaneshie Market, Accra, Ghana (Digital Address: GA-262-1811).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy applies to all individuals who:

  • Contact us by telephone, WhatsApp, email, or in person
  • Purchase or enquire about our products and services
  • Visit our website or social media platforms
  • Enter into any business relationship with Abak Company Limited
  • Apply for cold storage services or supply partnerships
ℹ️ By doing business with us or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from providing us with your personal information.
§2

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services, process orders, and communicate with you effectively. The categories of information we may collect include:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity Information: Your full name and, where applicable, your business name and registration details.
  • Contact Information: Phone number(s), WhatsApp number, email address, and physical or business address.
  • Order Information: Product selections, quantities, delivery preferences, and order history.
  • Payment Information: Payment method details (e.g., Mobile Money number, bank account details for invoice settlement). We do not store full card or banking credentials.
  • Communication Records: Records of calls, WhatsApp messages, emails, and in-person enquiries made to or from our team.
  • Business Information: For corporate clients — your company profile, procurement requirements, and relevant trade documentation.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website (if applicable), collected via cookies or analytics tools.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser used to access our digital platforms.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Information received from referrals, trade references, or partner organisations in connection with a business arrangement.
  • Public business information (e.g., company registration data from Ghana's Registrar-General's Department) used to verify business identity for commercial accounts.
§3

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Order Processing & Fulfilment: To receive, confirm, process, and deliver your orders for frozen food products.
  • Customer Service: To respond to your enquiries, complaints, and requests in a timely and professional manner.
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, delivery updates, invoices, receipts, and service-related notices via phone, WhatsApp, or email.
  • Account Management: To manage your account if you are a recurring business client, including tracking order history and payment records.
  • Payment Processing: To process and reconcile payments for goods and services supplied.
  • Marketing & Promotions: To send you information about new products, promotions, or services — but only where you have expressed interest or consented to receive such communications. You may opt out at any time.
  • Legal & Regulatory Compliance: To meet our obligations under Ghanaian law, including customs documentation, FDA compliance, and tax record-keeping.
  • Business Improvement: To analyse purchasing trends and feedback to improve our products, services, and customer experience.
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorised access, and other risks to our business and customers.
✅ We will never use your information for purposes beyond those listed above without first obtaining your explicit consent.
§4

Information Sharing & Disclosure

Abak Company Limited does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own commercial purposes. We may, however, share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers engaged to assist in delivery logistics, payment processing, or IT services — bound by confidentiality agreements and authorised only to use your data to perform services on our behalf.
  • Regulatory Authorities: Government agencies including Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA), Ghana FDA, Ghana Customs, and other regulators where disclosure is required by law.
  • Legal Proceedings: In connection with any legal claim, dispute resolution, or court order where disclosure is required.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets, your information may be transferred to the new business entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.
  • With Your Consent: For any purpose you have expressly agreed to.
⚠️ We require all third parties who handle your data on our behalf to maintain appropriate security measures and to use the information only for specified, legitimate purposes.
§5

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our measures include:

  • Restricting access to personal data to authorised Abak staff who need it to perform their duties.
  • Secure storage of physical and digital records containing personal information.
  • Regular staff awareness of data handling responsibilities.
  • Using secure, password-protected systems for storing customer and business data.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach that may affect your rights, we will notify affected individuals promptly and take corrective action in accordance with applicable law.

§6

Cookies & Website Tracking

If you visit our website, we may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect usage data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Understand how visitors navigate and use our website.
  • Improve website performance and content.

You may control cookie settings through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our website. We do not use cookies for invasive tracking or profiling purposes.

§7

Your Rights

As an individual whose personal data we process, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer necessary for us to retain it, subject to any legal obligations requiring retention.
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to the use of your personal data for marketing purposes at any time.
  • Portability: Request that we provide your personal data in a structured, commonly used format where technically feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12 of this Policy. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

📞 To make a data rights request, contact us at +233 24 729 8551 or +233 50 496 7111, or visit our office at HQ1 Anunsah Clottey Street, Kaneshie Market, Accra.
§8

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Our general retention guidelines are:

  • Customer Order Records: Retained for a minimum of 7 years in accordance with Ghanaian tax and accounting regulations.
  • Communication Records: Retained for up to 2 years from the last interaction, or longer if relevant to an ongoing business relationship.
  • Marketing Contacts: Retained until you opt out or request deletion, whichever comes first.
  • Regulatory Documents: Retained for the period required by the applicable Ghanaian law or regulatory authority.

When information is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymise it in a responsible manner.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit, as we have no control over their content or data practices.

Our products and services are intended for adults and business clients only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will take prompt steps to delete it. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

We reserve the right to update or amend this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or regulatory obligations. When we make material changes, we will:
